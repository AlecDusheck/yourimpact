Environmental Impact

When it comes to your personal environmental impact, there are many things you can do to reduce it. Some of the most important things you can do are

  • Reduce your energy consumption
  • Conserve water
  • Recycle and compost
  • Avoid products with excessive packaging
  • Buy local food when
  • Support sustainable agriculture

You can reduce your energy consumption by doing things like turning off lights when you leave a room, unplugging electronics when you're not using them, and using energy-efficient appliances. You can conserve water by fixing leaks, watering your plants during the cooler hours of the day, and using a rain barrel to collect rainwater. And you can recycle and compost by separating your trash into recyclables, compostables, and landfills.
